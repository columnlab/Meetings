Discussion

Wikipedia is an active site of knowledge production and consumption around the COVID-19 pandemic, but in unexpected ways → activity does not clearly track waves of infections/deaths

Editing strongly influenced by persistent editors rather than users-of-the-week

Content similarity suggests what has stabilized vs. what remains actively-edited

Limitations

English Wikipedia behavior only: largest and most active, but not representative of dynamics specific to other language editions and information needs

Over-emphasis on behavior of seed articles when pandemic shocks were dispersed

Page protection is a profound confounder for editing

No analysis of discussion, administrative, infrastructural, and off-wiki activity

Future work

Characterizing pandemic information seeking and updating in response to specific shocks

Using participation in COVID collaborations as a discontinuity on editor behavior

Understanding responses to efforts to introduce disinformation, racism, etc. and lessons for generalizing to other platforms
